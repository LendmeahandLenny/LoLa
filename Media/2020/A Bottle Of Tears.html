January 19, 2020

A Bottle Of Tears



I can not think of words that can describe how I feel both today and lately, words that can leave me feeling satisfied, that feeling that makes me write, to begin with, a feeling that can only be described as juicing oneself and then pouring that out into a bottle to be given to someone who cares to open it.
I am pouring out all of these things that are weighing me down, I am so tired, it is true, and I fear that it always will be.

I find myself racing against the time a lot more these days, despite this, I am making less progress than before, I find that funny, a laugh full of pity, I am tired.

My head is singing a song, one of which I cannot understand, but can feel and sympathize with, I can agree with all the cries and screams, I hear you from within, I am trying to fix it all, I do not know how, and I can not find it in me to ask for help, I am trying, for you, for us, and anyone else we find along the way.

The pulsing is making me sick, I thought I had everything figured out, but as I proceed, new things continue to jump out at me, and each time they shoot me back further, making each step back to where we left off, dreaded and painful, a trip I wish not to be a part of; I keep saying that by the end it will all make sense and be worth it, but I do not see myself making it there as of this moment.

I do not understand anything anymore, I am scared, I am desperate, but apparently not desperate enough to just compromise and make this work for me.
Why is it so hard for me to just get all of these things started?

I do not believe it to be too much in my basket, but the handle seems to be falling apart now, what am I to do?

I would like to allow myself the time to breathe, but I truly cannot afford to do that right now, there just seems to not be time for that, I am sorry, and I understand that all this is doing is making everything much worse, I just do not know where to start.

All this planning to waste time planning for what I can not plan for, that is how I spend my good days, why?
I need to take the leap, I can keep lying to myself saying that this is progress, but I see this as just being filler, something to keep things looking well, as it all falls apart behind the curtain.
Will I ever be able to make the leap? I want to, but I just cannot figure out where to start and I am tired of wasting time.
