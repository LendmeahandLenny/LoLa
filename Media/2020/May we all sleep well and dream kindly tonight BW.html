May we all sleep well and dream kindly tonight

A short post to welcome the night and sleepiness.


I decided to write a little tonight, I’ve been thinking a bit about “Me” and what that even means; how do you know if you’re you? Or what being you even feels like? I can’t say I don’t feel like “me” because I have not a clue as to what that feels like.

Hmm, I sit here, and I can describe the way the skin of my hand feels touching the face I’m in, behind? I don’t know, but I feel it, warm, a lot more firm than I’d expect something that can pass on a gentle touch would be.

If I focus too hard, I instead feel the skin of the face, the bone that lies behind it, pressed as close as can be, married to one another.
Though I can feel and describe these things, I can’t seem to be able to describe me, I find myself upset about this a lot, as of late, anyway, I never really paid any attention to it.

In other topics, I made my first self-portrait earlier today, I would like for it to have been better, but I am proud that I actually finished a picture for once, as a tiny little congratulations to myself, I decided it was good enough to set as my profile photos, though I’m a tad bit hesitant to post it on my Instagram… I feel like I should only post things I’m incredibly proud of, or find fitting for that, but I also made it a task to start posting to help find comfort and to stop being so hard on myself, I’m still kicking myself over the Christmas drawing.. oh well, I’ll figure it out I suppose. I need to remember to be kind to myself and actually give the mind and body time; patience is so hard to master. -[]
