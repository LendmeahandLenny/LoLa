Away I went for clarification I am ready And though it will be messy, I have realized that is just the way I am And there is nothing better than realization There is nothing better than the hope I see and feel today I write again, this time with wonder and a goal of making a path Through the woods, I shall creep As not to alert what is lurking I feel as though I am finally ready These things will take time, but time is all I have anyway. A path leading to the purple I see those beautiful fluffy clouds waiting for me I cannot express the joy just yet, but in secret, I shall whisper, in hopes, these words will bounce off the wall and land in the mind of a reader Sharing is all I have with this time And I am ready to share.
